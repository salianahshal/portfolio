Simulated Realities

Focused on the life simulation genre, this externship involved market research, player psychology analyses, and competitor research to provide strategic market expansion recommendations.

Background

The life simulation gaming genre has a dedicated player base and unique market dynamics. However, comprehensive research into player psychology and competitor analysis is limited. This externship aimed to provide strategic market expansion recommendations for Mobalytics within this genre.

Role

Gaming Market Research & Analytics Intern.

Time

4 weeks, May. - June, 2024

Affinity Map

Simulation games have evolved significantly by blending with other genres to create unique experiences, with sub-genres like life simulation, city-building, and survival simulation gaining traction. Games such as "BitLife" and "Fallout Shelter" illustrate this trend, highlighting the genre's versatility and growth. This affinity map offers a structured overview of simulation gaming genres, providing insights into market trends and strategic opportunities for expansion within the gaming industry.
